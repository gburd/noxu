  ops/s, ns/op  — throughput and latency

Known Noxu 1.0 gaps vs JE (affect benchmark fairness):
  * LockManager does not block threads — concurrent workload overhead understated
  * B-tree merge/compress not implemented — tree only grows, never shrinks
  * Abort does not undo WAL entries (no undo log yet)
